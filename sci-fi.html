Sci-Fi (Science Fiction) Genre

Science Fiction (Sci-Fi) anime deals with imaginative and futuristic concepts based on speculative science and technology. Common elements include space travel, advanced robotics, artificial intelligence, parallel universes, dystopian societies, and exploration of the consequences of scientific progress.

Key Characteristics:

  • Focus on Advanced Science and Technology
  • Often Set in Futuristic or Extraterrestrial Environments
  • Exploration of Speculative Concepts (AI, Cybernetics, Time Travel, etc.)
  • Themes Regarding Humanity's Future, Ethics, Society
  • Can Include Space Opera, Cyberpunk, Post-Apocalyptic subgenres
  • Frequently overlaps with Action, Mecha, Mystery, or Philosophical genres

Popular Examples:

  • Cowboy Bebop
  • Steins;Gate
  • Psycho-Pass
  • Ghost in the Shell (various series/films)
  • Legend of the Galactic Heroes
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion (also Mecha)
  • Ergo Proxy
  • Planetes
  • Serial Experiments Lain
