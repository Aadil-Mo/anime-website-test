Isekai Genre

Isekai (異世界), meaning "different world" or "otherworld," is a popular genre where the protagonist is transported to or reincarnated in another world, often one resembling a fantasy setting or video game. They must adapt to their new surroundings, often possessing unique abilities or knowledge from their previous life.

Key Characteristics:

  • Protagonist Transported/Reincarnated
  • Fantasy or Game-like World Setting
  • Often involves Magic, Monsters, Guilds
  • Protagonist may have Overpowered Abilities (OP MC)
  • Themes of Adaptation, Survival, Exploration

Popular Examples:

  • Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World
  • Sword Art Online (Arcs often fit)
  • That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime
  • KonoSuba: God's Blessing on this Wonderful World!
  • Overlord
  • No Game No Life
  • Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation
  • Log Horizon
  • The Rising of the Shield Hero
